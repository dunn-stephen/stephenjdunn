# EASTER_EGGS.md

## Hidden Extras

### 1. Startup Disk Secret
- Trigger: Open **About This Computer** from the Apple menu and click the Happy Mac icon 5 times.
- Result: The dialog swaps in a hidden message and marks the desktop easter-egg state as unlocked.

### 2. Calculator Joke Display
- Trigger: Open **Calculator** and enter `1337` or `80085`.
- Result: The display swaps to a joke label instead of the raw number.

### 3. Full House
- Trigger: Open every app at the same time:
  `Read Me`, `Resume`, `Mail`, `Space Invaders`, `Projects`, `Sherlock`, any `Note`, `Calculator`, and **About This Computer**.
- Result: A temporary desktop notification appears announcing that the whole machine is open at once.
